The Human Centered Design Charrette Workbook

One of the biggest parts of the department of HCDE is the human-centered design (HCD) Charrette. Initially developed by a professor within the department, the HCD Charrette is a fast-paced design activity that propagates collaboration and teaches critical thinking skills. Not only do we utilize these workshops in our undergraduate and graduate classrooms, but HCDE also conducts outreach programs that have since introduced design charrettes to K-12 students all across the Puget Sound Area.
