It's Nice to Meet You!


Hi, my name is Valerie Remaker and I am a junior studying Human Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE), at the University of Washington. Does that actually count as engineering? Yes! An engineer is a person who designs and builds machines and structures. As a Human Centered Designer, I design and build products and experiences. As Designers and Engineers, we design for human needs and desires, rather than designing for optimized machines and or algorithms. Our field has a strong focus on empathy to get to the root of users' needs and to design innovative and easily understood solutions to complex human problems. In the past few quarters I have had the pleasure of working within our department to bring HCDE ideas to K-12 students who are interested in engineering, through the introduction of a fast paced design charrette workshop. Through my outreach work, I hope to fight the stereotype that engineers have to be lonely nerds who are good at math and to inspire students to use their creativity to solve real world problems.
I plan to use my degree to pursue user experience design and web development.
